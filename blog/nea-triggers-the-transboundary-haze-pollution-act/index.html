NEA triggers the Transboundary Haze Pollution Act

Minister for the Environment Mr Vivian Balakrishnan has announced details on his Facebook account of the first companies to be served notice under the Transboundary Haze Pollution Act. The Minister also announced the government’s intentions to review its procurement procedure saying they would be taking measures to ensure where possible their suppliers followed internationally recognised sustainability criteria.
